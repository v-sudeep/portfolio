I'm an aspiring Data Analyst and Computer Science Engineer who enjoys the part of analytics most people skip past: getting the data clean enough, and the story clear enough, that a business actually changes course because of it.

My work spans Python, SQL, Power BI, and Tableau, with a growing focus on machine learning for prediction — from forecasting employee attrition to unpacking what actually drives e-commerce revenue. I like dashboards that answer the next three questions someone will ask, not just the first one.

Outside of dashboards, I co-authored a published research paper on a blockchain-based voting system — proof that I'm as comfortable with cryptographic protocols as I am with a pivot table.
